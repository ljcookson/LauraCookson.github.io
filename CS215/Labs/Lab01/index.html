About Laura

I moved to Saskatchewan in 2008 from British Columbia, where I was born and raised in Penticton. I graduated high school before many of my classmates were even conceived, in June 2000. I moved to the greater Vancouver area in April 2004. I graduated from BCIT (British Columbia Institute of Technology) with a DiplTech in FinMan (Diploma of Technology in Financial Management with a Professional Accounting major) in 2006. I worked in the accounting industry for ten years before deciding to return for a Bachelor's degree. I worked full-time while attending part-time for three years and began attending school full-time in Fall 2019.

I chose a Computer Science degree over a Business degree because I have always been interested in technology and thought it would provide me with the most career options. My original plan was to use it as the entry requirement for the CPA (Certified Professional Accountant) program. Still, I have started considering a potential career in teaching at the University Level.
